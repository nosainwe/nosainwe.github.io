About Me

I'm a first-year Erasmus Mundus MSc student in Intelligent Photonics for Security, Reliability & Safety (iPSRS), currently at the University of Eastern Finland in Joensuu, with specialisation in Biometrics and Intelligent Vision. Before this, I completed a First Class BEng in Electrical & Electronics Engineering at the University of Benin, graduating in the top 5% of my class.

My work spans the gap between optics and AI. On the photonics side, I'm drawn to spectroscopy-driven diagnostics and optical design. On the ML side, I build practical systems: detection pipelines, unsupervised anomaly detectors, spectral classifiers. I publish on GitHub under the username nosainwe, with clean, typed Python code and MIT-licensed repos. Currently seeking a summer internship or MSc thesis placement in biomedical spectroscopy or intelligent vision.
